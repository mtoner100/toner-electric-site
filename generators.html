To Our Valued Customers

It is with a heavy heart that I write to inform you of the closure of our Generator Division at Toner Electric, effective 04/24/2025. This decision comes after the recent and unexpected passing of our Department head, Jeffrey Belli, aka "Generator Jeff", whose contributions, leadership, and spirit were central to our operations and our mission.

This loss has deeply affected us all, both personally and professionally. After careful consideration, we have determined that the most respectful and appropriate course of action is to close our Generator Department and honor the legacy he left behind.

We are immensely grateful for the support, trust, and relationships we've built over the years. Serving you has been our privilege, and your loyalty has meant more to us than words can express.

We highly recommend you contact one of these Certified Generac Dealers to establish a relationship and service contract. We recommend all three in no particular order:

  • Chris Electric – Newport, RI – (401) 847-2542
  • Sunshine Energy – Bristol, RI – (401) 253-7781
  • Ralco Generator – Westport, MA – (508) 938-8550

For any outstanding matters, please feel free to reach out to me directly at scott@tonerelectric.com or (401) 847-0993. We will do our best to ensure a smooth transition and address any remaining concerns.

With sincere appreciation,
Scott Toner
Vice President
Toner Electric Co Inc.
