Questions & Answers

1. Does my generator need to be maintained annually?

Yes! Generators require periodic oil and filter changes to ensure maximum performance for years of reliable service.

2. What are the advantages of purchasing and having Toner Electric do the installation?
  • Installation by a Generac factory trained technician
  • Generator will be registered and logged as being installed by a certified Generac dealer
  • Prompt installation and your home or business will be left clean and neat
3. What does the maintenance light on my generator mean?
  • Green Light = Generator is ready to run.
  • Yellow Light = Maintenance Reminder. Time to schedule an appointment.
  • Red Light = Generator will not operate
